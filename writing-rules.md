# Writing rules

Follow these rules for all copy, UI text, descriptions, marketing content, and comments written in this project.

---

## Voice

Write like a person. Short sentences. Direct claims. No filler.

UK English throughout. Use -ise not -ize. Colour not color. Whilst not while. Practise (verb) not practice.

No em dashes. Use a comma, a full stop, or rewrite as two sentences.

No ellipsis for effect. If a thought is unfinished, finish it.

---

## Words to watch in clusters

These are fine on their own. If two or more appear in the same paragraph, rewrite.

harness, navigate, foster, elevate, empower, streamline, bolster, resonate, revolutionise, facilitate, nuanced, crucial, ecosystem, myriad, cultivate, illuminate, reimagine

---

## Sentence patterns to avoid

**"It's not X, it's Y"** -- rewrite as a direct positive statement.

**Hollow intensifiers** -- cut "genuine", "truly", "quite frankly", "to be honest", "let's be clear", "it's worth noting that". State the fact directly.

**Vague endorsements** -- cut "worth reading", "worth exploring", "worth checking out". Say why something matters instead.

**Hedging** -- cut "perhaps", "could potentially", "it's important to note that", "to be clear". Make the point.

**Filler conclusions** -- cut "the future looks bright", "only time will tell", "as we move forward". Say something specific or nothing.

**Rule of three** -- vary groupings. Use two items, four items, or a full sentence. One "adjective, adjective, and adjective" pattern per piece maximum.

---

## Formatting

No bold overuse. One bolded phrase per major section at most. If something is important enough to bold, restructure the sentence to lead with it instead.

No emoji in headings.

Convert bullet-heavy sections into prose where possible. Bullets only for genuine lists: step-by-step instructions, feature comparisons, parameter lists.

---

## App Store copy specifically

Character limits are hard limits, not targets. Count before submitting.

Write the first three lines of any description as if nothing after them will be read. That is all most people see.

Use real words from the Zazu word bank in screenshots and examples. Mellifluous, Ephemeral, Susurrus, Penumbra. The words are part of the brand.

Do not repeat keywords already in the app name or subtitle. Apple and Google index those automatically.

---

## Quick checklist before committing any copy

- No em dashes
- No words from the "never use" list
- No hollow intensifiers
- UK English spellings
- First sentence states the point directly
- No filler conclusion
